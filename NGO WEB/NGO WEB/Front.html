Welcome to HELPING HANDS, where compassion meets action. Our mission is to create positive, lasting change in the lives of those who need it most. With unwavering dedication, we tackle pressing global issues such as poverty, education, healthcare, and environmental sustainability. Our team of passionate individuals, volunteers, and supporters work tirelessly to make a meaningful impact in communities around the world. Together, we strive to build a brighter future for all, one step at a time. Join us in our journey towards a more compassionate and equitable world. Together, we can make a difference that lasts a lifetime.

Our Mission

Helping Hands Foundation, is a 501(c)(3) charitable organization whose mission is to alleviate poverty and hunger in the continent of India. We work to feed, empower, cloth, and provide basic amenities such as food, water, electricity, housing, and healthcare to the poorest of India.
