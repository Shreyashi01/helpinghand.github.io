With a population of 140 Billion, last mile delivery of healthcare in our country has always been a challenge. Nearly 72% of the population lives in rural areas and has access to only about 25% of the healthcare infrastructure.

WHAT WE DO


Smile Foundations comprehensive and community-centric health programme takes primary healthcare services to the doorsteps of underserved communities in both rural and urban India. Following a two pronged approach, the programme provides curative as well as preventive services, addressing the gaps in availability, accessibility and affordability of healthcare.

OUR FOCUS


Providing need-based healthcare services to specific target groups through standalone health camps with specialized doctors, point of care tests, and sometimes surgical facilities
